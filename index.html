Hackathon 2020

I participated in Calgary Hacks' 2020 Hackathon which took place at the University of Calgary. The topic for my teams project was a video game that focuses on aspects of Calgary. My team built our game using JavaScript. The game focused on Calgary's transit more so on the LRT system. The game would simulate public transit routes and the player would have to develop a train system from scratch to transport citizens throughout the city. The game was to be created in 24 hours and presented to the panel of judges at the end of the time limit.
